# Fetch & Boxed Website UI/UX

A website UI/UX design concept for **Fetch & Boxed**, a pet subscription box brand created for dog owners who want stylish, convenient, and customizable pet apparel and accessories. This project was designed using **Canva** as a website mockup and e-commerce concept.

## About the Project

**Fetch & Boxed** is a pet subscription website concept that offers curated and customizable subscription boxes for small, medium, and large dogs. The brand focuses on dog apparel and accessories such as bandanas, collars, leashes, hoodies, sweaters, raincoats, charms, and themed add-ons.

The goal of this design is to create a simple, friendly, and pet-focused shopping experience where customers can easily choose a box based on their dog’s size, style preference, and subscription needs.

## Design Concept

The website was designed with a clean, playful, and approachable layout that fits a modern pet brand. The design uses a fresh green color palette to create a natural, friendly, and trustworthy look.

The main concept highlights:

* Easy box selection
* Dog size-based subscription options
* Tiered box plans
* Pet-friendly visuals
* Clear call-to-action sections
* Simple e-commerce navigation
* A warm and playful brand identity

## Subscription Box Options

Fetch & Boxed includes different subscription box tiers designed for various dog sizes and customer preferences.

### Basic Box

Includes simple everyday accessories such as:

* Bandana or bowtie
* Matching collar and leash
* Size-based accessory options for small, medium, and large dogs

### Deluxe Box

Includes upgraded accessories such as:

* Collar or harness
* Bandana
* Matching add-on accessory such as a charm, poop bag holder, reflective clip, leash pouch, or tag

### Premium Box

Includes more complete apparel and accessory combinations such as:

* Hoodie, sweater, raincoat, jacket, vest, coat, or poncho
* Matching collar
* Bandana
* Surprise accessory

### Birthday Box

A special themed box option for dog birthdays, including items such as:

* “Birthday Pup” shirt or hoodie
* Birthday bandana
* Party hat, bow, crown, or themed accessory
* Keepsake item such as a pawprint card or plush

## Customization Features

The concept includes customization options to make each box more personalized for the customer and their dog.

Possible customization options include:

* Dog size selection: Small, Medium, or Large
* Breed or measurement-based sizing guidance
* Preferred color palette selection such as pastel, vibrant, or earthy tones
* Optional personalized name tag or charm
* Optional Birthday Box add-on
* Quarterly subscription delivery option
