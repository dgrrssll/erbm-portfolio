# Social Media Graphic Design for Limesdry Cleaners

**Client:** Limesdry Cleaners
**Project Type:** Social Media Graphic Design
**Role:** Graphic Designer

Created weekly social media graphics for Limesdry Cleaners to support their online content and brand presence. The graphics were designed to make their services more visually appealing, consistent, and engaging for social media audiences.

## Key Work

* Created weekly social media graphics for a cleaning service brand
* Designed clean and professional layouts for online posting
* Used readable text and clear visual hierarchy
* Created graphics to support brand awareness and service promotion
* Prepared social media-ready designs for regular posting
