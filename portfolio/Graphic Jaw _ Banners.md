# Facebook Timeline Banner Design for Graphic Jaw

**Client:** Graphic Jaw
**Project Type:** Social Media Banner Design
**Role:** Graphic Designer

Created a Facebook timeline cover banner for Graphic Jaw to enhance the brand’s social media presence and establish a strong visual identity. The banner was designed to be eye-catching, professional, and consistent with the brand’s overall style, helping create a more polished and engaging page presentation.

## Key Work

* Created a Facebook timeline cover banner for Graphic Jaw
* Designed a visually appealing and brand-aligned layout
* Used clear typography and balanced visual composition
* Strengthened the brand’s identity and social media presence
* Prepared a social media-ready design optimized for Facebook timeline cover use
