# Google Ads Design for Graphic Jaw

**Client:** Graphic Jaw
**Project Type:** Google Ads Graphic Design
**Role:** Graphic Designer

Created Google ad graphics for Graphic Jaw’s client products to support online promotions and increase brand visibility. The ads were designed to be visually appealing, clear, and aligned with the product branding to help capture attention and encourage potential customers to take action.

## Key Work

* Created Google ad graphics for client products
* Designed clear and attention-grabbing promotional layouts
* Used strong visual hierarchy and readable text
* Maintained consistency with the product’s branding
* Prepared ad-ready graphics optimized for online campaigns
