# YouTube Thumbnail Design for Shelton Powell

Client: Shelton Powell
Project Type: YouTube Thumbnail Design
Role: Graphic Designer/Video Editor


Designed YouTube thumbnails for Shelton Powell's video content. The thumbnails were created to support clear visual branding, make each topic easier to understand at a glance, and help the videos appear more engaging to viewers.

# Key Work:

- Created thumbnail layouts for YouTube videos
- Used strong visual hierarchy and readable text
- Designed graphics aligned with the video topic and brand style
- Prepared thumbnails for online publishing
