# Shopify Product Image Design for Graphic Jaw

**Client:** Graphic Jaw
**Project Type:** E-commerce Product Image Design
**Role:** Graphic Designer

Created product images for Graphic Jaw’s Shopify store to present the client’s products in a clear, professional, and visually appealing way. The designs were developed to improve product presentation, maintain visual consistency, and support a more polished online shopping experience.

## Key Work

* Created product images for a Shopify e-commerce store
* Designed clean and visually consistent product layouts
* Highlighted important product features and details
* Strengthened the overall presentation of the online store
* Prepared Shopify-ready graphics for product listings
