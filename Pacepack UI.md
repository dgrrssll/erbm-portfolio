# PacePack Website UI/UX

A website UI/UX design concept for **PacePack**, a curated runner gift box brand created for marathon runners, athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and gift-givers. This project was designed as a Shopify-style e-commerce website concept.

## About the Project

**PacePack** is a runner gift box website concept that offers curated one-time kits for customers who want race-day essentials, recovery tools, motivational items, and fitness accessories in one ready-to-gift package.

The goal of this design is to create a simple, energetic, and easy-to-use shopping experience for runners and gift-givers who want convenient access to running essentials, recovery items, and motivational products.

## Design Concept

The website was designed with a clean, modern, and athletic layout that fits a runner-focused e-commerce brand. The design uses an energetic color palette to create a sporty, motivational, and professional look.

The main concept highlights:

* Easy runner kit selection
* One-time gift box purchase option
* Tiered runner pack options
* Race-day and recovery-focused products
* Clear call-to-action sections
* Simple e-commerce navigation
* A motivational and athletic brand identity

## Box Options

PacePack includes different runner kit tiers designed for various customer needs, budgets, and gifting purposes.

### PacePack Start Line Pack

Includes basic race-day and running essentials such as:

* Energy gels or chews
* Electrolyte drink mix or tablet pack
* Travel-size anti-chafe balm
* Moisture-wicking running socks
* SPF lip balm
* Motivational card

### PacePack Performance Pack

Includes upgraded running and recovery items such as:

* Everything in the Start Line Pack
* Cooling towel or neck gaiter
* Recovery snack
* Mini first-aid kit or blister pack
* Small Epsom salt pack or bath bomb
* Branded sticker or thank-you card

### PacePack Runner’s High Pack

Includes premium runner gift items such as:

* Everything in the Performance Pack
* Mini instant camera or equivalent
* Instant film
* Compression socks
* Portable massage ball or mini roller
* Digital QR code access to stretching guides, playlists, and motivational content

## Customization Features

The concept includes simple curated options to make each PacePack more useful and appealing for different customers.

Possible customization options include:

* Choice of runner kit tier
* One-time purchase model
* Seasonal or limited-edition kit options
* Optional add-ons or upgrades
* Gift-ready packaging
* Digital QR code access for selected packs
* Branded inserts, stickers, or motivational cards
