# PacePack Website UI/UX

A website UI/UX design concept for **PacePack**, a curated marathon runner gift box brand created for runners, athletes, fitness enthusiasts, and gift-givers. This project focuses on building a clean, energetic, and conversion-friendly e-commerce website concept for one-time runner kits.

## About the Project

**PacePack** is an e-commerce gift box concept designed for marathon runners and active individuals who need race-day essentials, recovery tools, motivation, and useful fitness accessories in one ready-to-gift package.

Unlike a subscription-based model, PacePack focuses on **one-time ordered kits** with possible seasonal or limited-edition releases. Each box is designed to support runners before, during, and after their race or training journey.

The goal of this design is to create a modern, motivational, and easy-to-use shopping experience where customers can quickly choose the right runner kit based on their needs, budget, or gifting purpose.

## Brand Concept

The brand identity of PacePack is built around movement, endurance, preparation, and motivation.

The name **PacePack** reflects the idea of helping runners stay prepared and supported throughout their fitness journey. The design direction combines athletic energy with a stylish and modern e-commerce look.

The brand concept highlights:

* Running and fitness lifestyle
* Race-day preparation
* Recovery and wellness
* Motivation and encouragement
* Gift-ready curated boxes
* Clean and modern online shopping experience

## Design Concept

The website design uses a clean, energetic, and professional layout that fits a runner-focused e-commerce brand. The design is intended to feel motivational, easy to navigate, and visually engaging for athletes and gift buyers.

The website concept includes:

* Strong homepage hero section
* Clear product box presentation
* Easy navigation menu
* Video ad or brand storytelling section
* “What’s Inside the Box” product preview
* Simple shopping cart access
* Clear call-to-action buttons
* Mobile-friendly e-commerce layout
* Trust-building sections such as FAQs, reviews, and contact options

## Product Box Tiers

PacePack includes three main runner kit tiers designed for different types of customers.

### PacePack Start Line Pack

Designed for first-time marathoners, beginner runners, or budget-conscious buyers.

Possible contents include:

* Energy gels or chews
* Electrolyte drink mix or tablet pack
* Travel-size anti-chafe balm
* Moisture-wicking running socks
* SPF lip balm
* Motivational card

### PacePack Performance Pack

Designed for experienced runners, active customers, or gift-givers looking for a more complete runner kit.

Possible contents include everything in the Start Line Pack, plus:

* Cooling towel or neck gaiter
* Recovery snack
* Mini first-aid kit or blister pack
* Epsom salt pack or bath bomb
* Branded sticker or thank-you card

### PacePack Runner’s High Pack

Designed for marathon veterans, influencers, corporate wellness gifts, or premium gifting.

Possible contents include everything in the Performance Pack, plus:

* Mini instant camera or equivalent
* Instant film
* Compression socks
* Portable massage ball or mini roller
* Digital QR code access to stretching guides, playlists, and motivational content

## Website Structure

The PacePack website concept includes a simple and user-friendly structure.

### Home

Introduces the brand, explains the purpose of PacePack, and highlights the value of curated runner gift boxes.

### Gift It / Runner Kits

Displays the main product box tiers with clear details about what is included, who each pack is best for, and the value of each kit.

### Challenges

An optional motivational section where customers can join running or fitness challenges and unlock rewards or discounts.

### FAQ

Answers common questions about box contents, shipping, returns, customization, and digital rewards.

### Contact Us

Provides easy access to customer support through email, contact forms, or live chat.
