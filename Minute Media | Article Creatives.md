# Article Creative Design for Minute Media

**Client:** Minute Media
**Project Type:** Article Creative Design
**Role:** Graphic Designer

Created article creatives for Minute Media to support high-engagement online articles. The graphics were designed to make article topics more visually appealing, attention-grabbing, and easier for viewers to understand at a glance.

## Key Work

* Created graphics for article-based online content
* Designed attention-grabbing visuals for entertainment and trending topics
* Created creatives for topics such as celebrities, illnesses, banned toys, and other high-interest stories
* Used clear visual hierarchy to make the topic easy to understand quickly
* Prepared graphics for digital publishing and audience engagement
